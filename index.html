What is the manufacturing cost?
the manufacturing cost is a n important part for understanding the productivity and profitability of a business. Basically it is the sum of cash that has been spend on production activities during a specific financial period, simply it is the cost of manufacturing products.
How to calculate manufacturing cost?
To calculate manufacturing cost you should use the following formula:
manufacturing cost = direct materials + direct labotr + manufacturing overhead
where:
direct materials = beginning raw materials + cost of raw materials purchased - ending raw materials inventory
Why use the manufacturing cost?
the manufacturing cost is used to adjust the selling price of a product and to indentify and cut the extra expenses.
